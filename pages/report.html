Report an Incident

Help your community by reporting disaster incidents, hazards, or areas that need attention.

Important: For emergencies requiring immediate assistance, please call 911 instead of using this form.
Maximum 3 photos, 5MB each

Why Report Incidents?

Your reports help us identify patterns, allocate resources, and take preventive measures against future disasters.

What Happens After Reporting

  1. Your report is reviewed by our team
  2. Information is shared with relevant authorities and surrounding communities
  3. The incident is tracked and monitored

Tips for Effective Reporting

  • Be as specific as possible about the location
  • Include photos when available
  • Provide contact information for follow-up
  • Report as soon as it's safe to do so
